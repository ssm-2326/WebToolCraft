Explore Our Powerful Online Tools

Boost your productivity and accuracy with our wide range of online utility tools designed for writers, students, developers, and professionals.

Word & Character Counter

This tool allows you to count the number of words, characters, and even paragraphs in your text. Whether you're writing for an assignment, social media, or professional documentation, it provides accurate metrics in real time. Simply paste your content and see the stats update instantly.

Age Calculator

Quickly find out your exact age in years, months, and days with our age calculator. Perfect for forms, records, and birthday countdowns. It's simple and intuitive—just enter your date of birth and get accurate results.

BMI Calculator

Monitor your health with our Body Mass Index calculator. Input your height and weight, and instantly learn if you fall in the underweight, normal, overweight, or obese category. This tool is essential for health tracking and fitness planning.

Case Converter

Transform your text into different cases such as UPPERCASE, lowercase, Sentence case, or Capitalized Case. It's a handy tool for formatting content for professional documents, titles, and online publishing.

Remove Extra Spaces

Clean up your text by removing unnecessary white spaces. Great for content editing, coding, or preparing text for web publishing. This tool ensures your content is tidy and properly formatted.

Slug Generator

Create SEO-friendly URL slugs from your titles or phrases. This tool helps improve your website's visibility and makes your URLs cleaner and easier to read.

Reading Time Estimator

Estimate how long it will take to read your text. Useful for blog posts, essays, or articles to give your readers a quick preview of reading time before they begin.

Password Generator

Generate secure and strong passwords instantly. You can customize the length and characters to get a password that's both unique and hard to crack—essential for online safety and data protection.

Character Counter (No Spaces)

Count only the characters excluding spaces. Ideal for coding, writing tweet-length content, or input fields that require character-limited inputs without white spaces.

Line Counter

Know exactly how many lines your content has. It's particularly useful for developers and writers working with scripts, code, or poetry.
